Projects

Our projects are aligned to the UN SDGs. We work on projects in self-organising teams. As the projects gain momentum, the teams grow and evolve to stay efficient and effective. Sometimes, the nature of a project means that a new organisation is formed, and spun out of the community.

Interested in one of the projects below? Join Otio on Slack and start contributing. We look forward to your input!

Anyone can propose a new project, recruit the support that they need, and get to work. Our projects are actively recruiting new contributors, including projects with a status of 'backlog'. If you have any questions about projects, how to join them, how they're run, or you would like to join a specific project that has caught your eye, go ahead and put out a message in the #projects channel on Slack.

Note

The best way to get involved with Otio is via our Slack workspace. If you have any questions that you'd like to ask without joining Slack, please email us on hello@otio.work
